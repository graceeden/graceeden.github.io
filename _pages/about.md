Hello, I am Grace Eden I'm currently a professor in Bengaluru, India. I'm a  researcher and educator in user experience and interaction design. The focus of my research is to understand how new technologies transform social life, people’s day-to-day interactions, and the effects of technology on human behaviour, communication and social
interaction. A key objective in my work is to understand how complex systems might be successfully embedded into a variety of settings through the co-design of solutions with stakeholders.

**Research interests: **
Design Methodology, Participatory Design, Human-Computer Interaction, and Design Futures.
